Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

SV Trucking Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") operates the Smart Truck Driver mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

With your permission, we collect precise location data to:

  • Provide real-time route tracking
  • Support delivery navigation
  • Enable dispatch management
  • Generate analytics on driver efficiency

2.2 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Driver license details
  • Vehicle information
  • Authentication credentials

2.3 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the application:

  • Shipments processed
  • Deliveries completed
  • Application performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns

2.4 Camera & Photos

With your permission, we collect photos for proof of delivery and loading documentation.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the application
  • To process shipments and deliveries
  • To communicate with you regarding your account
  • To improve application performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating the application
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Your employer or company that deployed this application
  • Firebase and Google services for push notifications and analytics

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in a jurisdiction with GDPR or similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@svtrucking.biz

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service and comply with legal obligations. Location data is typically retained for 12 months.

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence. By using this application, you consent to such transfers.

10. Third-Party Services

This application uses third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google) - Analytics and push notifications
  • Google Maps - Location and mapping services
  • Payment processors - For transaction processing
